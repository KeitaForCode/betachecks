TRAINING




Betachecks is deploying an open and ever growing trainings platform providing access to curated contents related to fact checking, media literacy and accountability, media freedom and pluralism in Africa. Training items range from different forms of media reports and news sources, from media tools to practical instruments such as trainings and manuals, as well as opportunities for media professionals and young journalists. Our trainings are classified into courses (face to face & online courses), workshops and webinars on topics related to fact checking, media literacy, press and media freedom and pluralism. Opportunities for capacity building are also freely available online and on demand.
