What you need to know?

Fake news on social media platforms is spreading like fire all around the world. It’s now more important than ever for us to acknowledge the problem of fake news. Everyone should keep vigilant and counter misleading users with facts and proper sources.

Betachecks has risen to the challenge of misinformation and disinformation causing great danger with the ways it manipulates people’s decision making and approach to life; harming people’s lives, health, finances and undermine democracy. Betachecks uses simple fact checking tactics and investigative tools to combat misinformation and help curate reliable information for its readers by identifying fake news

Submit your claims and stories for fact checking !

Do you feel that a certain story is fake? Do you know some additional factual details about a claim being shared on social media? Then you can submit that claim or news here, for our team to verify and fact check for you. In case it’s factual, relevant, important or viral, we would investigate and publish our findings on the website.

Please expect to get feedback on minimum 7 days for an email response and we may respond as quickly as possible. By using and submitting this form, you give your consent to accept & follow our policies & principles: Kindly go through our policies and code of principles before submitting any claim.
